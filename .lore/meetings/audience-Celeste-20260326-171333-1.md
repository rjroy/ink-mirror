Celeste reviewed the ink-mirror vision document with the user and worked through the seven open questions systematically. The discussion balanced resolving design decisions with identifying questions requiring external research before implementation.

Three questions were resolved through direct discussion. The Observer will run automatically on submit to minimize friction, with observations available for review on demand rather than pushed to the user. Contradictions within the style profile will be surfaced to the user during curation, allowing them to decide whether patterns represent intentional modes or drift. The CLI will be treated as a first-class client rendering the same journal, observations, and profile through the terminal interface rather than a secondary feature added later.

Four questions were deferred to research. The Observer's historical comparison window (how much prior corpus to analyze) requires investigation into token strategies, RAG approaches, and precedent from similar tools. Observation granularity (the right level of abstraction from raw metrics to actionable patterns) depends on research constrained by the frictionless principle. The minimum viable observation set (which 2-3 dimensions to ship first) will likely be informed by granularity research. Profile versioning and voice evolution over time requires research into linguistic studies on how writing changes over time.

The vision document was updated to add a Principles section naming "frictionless" and "clients are views" as governing design philosophy. Resolved questions were moved into their own section with rationale. Open questions were reorganized with cross-references to four research issues. The architecture section clarified that CLI is first-class and that observations run automatically rather than on user request.

Four research issues were created and linked: research-observer-history-window.md, research-observation-granularity.md, research-minimum-viable-observation.md, and research-profile-versioning.md. The vision document was updated and locked pending research outcomes, which are expected to prompt revision of the observation pipeline and profile versioning sections.
