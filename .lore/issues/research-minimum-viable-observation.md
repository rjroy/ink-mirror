# Research: Minimum Viable Observation Set

## Question

The Observer could analyze dozens of dimensions. Which two or three matter most for a first version that proves the concept?

## Notes

This likely falls out of the granularity research (research-observation-granularity.md). Once we understand what dimensions exist and how to measure them, the MVP set becomes a filtering question rather than a discovery question.

## Constraints

- Must prove the core loop: write, observe, curate, apply
- Observations must be concrete enough to curate ("is this intentional?")
- The MVP set should cover different categories (e.g., one structural, one tonal) so the range of observation types is visible early
